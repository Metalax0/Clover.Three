 THREE

Three leaves of a clover, three empty canvases, three novice developers, three curious minds. Welcome to project THREE. A project set up by three Computing students who have no clue of what they are doing, that are working together to build a simple front end oriented website.

Word Counter
AND Coin Flip


The Word Counter program takes in user's input in a text field and gives out word count, letter count and sentence count as a result. The Coin Flip program, on a click of a button will give the output of either heads or tails along with their occurance frequency. These programs are present in Dr.Senior page of the project.

To-Do-List
AND Puzzle


The To-Do-List program, helps user keep track of their activities by setting tasks, adding new ones, marking them as complete and finally deleting them. The Puzzle program is a crptogram where user must decrypt the encoded text message and solve different stages of the puzzle. These programs are present in Metalax page of the project.
